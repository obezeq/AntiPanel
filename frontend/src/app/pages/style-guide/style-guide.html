Colors

Dark mode color palette. Click any color to copy its hex value.

AntiPanel embraces darkness as its foundation. Where traditional panels overwhelm with visual noise, we strip away distractions. Our monochromatic palette creates depth through subtle gradations—from pure black to soft grays—allowing content to breathe and actions to speak clearly. Accent colors are used with surgical precision: green confirms, red alerts, yellow indicates progress, and blue guides. This is the anti-panel aesthetic: minimal, confident, and unapologetically focused.
